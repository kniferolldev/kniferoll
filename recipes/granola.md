# Granola

## Ingredients

- oats - 450 g
- nuts - 225 g
- pumpkin seeds - 105 g, raw
- unsweetened coconut flakes - 90 g
- salt - 15 g
- brown sugar - 120 g
- maple syrup - 105 g
- olive oil - 90 g
- dried fruit - 210 g

## Steps

1. In a large bowl, combine [[oats]], [[nuts]], [[pumpkin-seeds]], and
   [[unsweetened-coconut-flakes]].
2. In a saucepan or microwave, melt or dissolve [[salt]], [[brown-sugar]],
   [[maple-syrup]], and [[olive-oil]] over gentle heat until uniform.
3. Pour the liquid mixture into the dry ingredients and mix thoroughly to coat
   evenly.
4. Spread the mixture onto a parchment-lined sheet pan.
5. Bake @300F for @40m, stirring or turning halfway through.
6. Cool slightly, then mix in [[dried-fruit]].

## Notes

Makes enough to fit one ~4qt Oxo container. Bake until golden brown and crisp;
mixture will firm up further as it cools.
